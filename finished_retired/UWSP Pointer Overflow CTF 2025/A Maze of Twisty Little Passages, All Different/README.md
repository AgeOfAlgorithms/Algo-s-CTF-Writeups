## Challenge Name
A Maze of Twisty Little Passages, All Different

## Platform
UWSP Pointer Overflow CTF 2025

## Category
Misc

## Difficulty
easy-medium

## Challenge Description
A telemetry drone sent into the ruins beneath Knossos Station returned with its mapping feed corrupted. The only thing that survived is the movement log — a string of position updates, turns, and dead ends. Your task is to reconstruct what the drone saw and find the message hidden in its path.

File: labyrinth.log
Format: plain text telemetry.
Goal: interpret the path, reconstruct the maze layout, and read the word(s) it spells.

Flag format: poctf{uwsp_[THE HIDDEN MESSAGE]}

[MISSION LOG]
Probe: ARIADNE-1
Note: Start facing NORTH. Grid units are 1×1. “Step forward” moves one cell in the current facing.
Note: Every 10 moves, the probe performs ORIENTATION CALIBRATION (reports current facing).
Symbols in narrative:
- “No forward possible” means a wall; the next line often indicates a turn.
- “Returning to previous junction” is flavor; position does not change unless followed by steps/turns.
- “Turning 180 degrees” reverses facing.
